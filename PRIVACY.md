# Privacy Policy for Browser Launcher Pro

Last updated: [Current Date]

## Introduction

Browser Launcher Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data
- Browser version information (only when version tracking is enabled)
- Extension settings and preferences
- WSL configuration (if enabled)

### We Do Not Collect
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Search queries
- Location data
- Any other personal or sensitive information

## How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:
- Provide the extension's core functionality
- Store your preferences locally
- Display browser version information
- Enable WSL integration features

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not:
- Transmit data to external servers
- Share information with third parties
- Store data in the cloud

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your locally stored data
- Delete all extension data
- Disable features that collect information
- Uninstall the extension at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
